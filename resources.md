## Theoretical Foundations Referenced

- Kobasa, S.C. (1979). Hardiness Theory — commitment, control, challenge as protective attitudes
- Antonovsky, A. (1979). Sense of Coherence — comprehensibility, manageability, meaningfulness
- Bandura, A. (1988). Self-Efficacy Theory — confidence in ability to influence life events
- Neuropsychology: amygdala/prefrontal cortex inverse relationship during stress response
